# SOP: Campaign Planning & Execution

## Tujuan
Merancang dan mengeksekusi kampanye marketing yang terintegrasi dari awareness hingga conversion.

## Framework Kampanye

### Fase 1: Research & Strategy (Minggu 1)
1. **Market Research**
   - Analisis kompetitor
   - Identifikasi target audiens
   - Tren industri terkini
   - Seasonal opportunity mapping

2. **Campaign Brief**
   - Objective (SMART goals)
   - Target audience persona
   - Key message & positioning
   - Budget allocation
   - Timeline

3. **Channel Strategy**
   - Platform selection
   - Content mix per platform
   - Paid vs organic ratio
   - KOL selection criteria

### Fase 2: Creative Development (Minggu 2)
1. **Campaign Concept**
   - Tagline kampanye
   - Campaign narrative
   - Message communication hierarchy
   - Visual mood board

2. **Content Production**
   - Photo/video shooting
   - Graphic design assets
   - Landing page (jika diperlukan)
   - Ad creatives

3. **KOL Briefing**
   - KOL listing & approach
   - Brief document
   - Content guidelines
   - Timeline koordinasi

### Fase 3: Activation (Minggu 3-4)
1. **Organic Activation**
   - Content publishing sesuai calendar
   - Community engagement
   - Cross-platform promotion

2. **Paid Activation**
   - Meta Ads setup (audience, placement, budget)
   - Google Ads setup (jika applicable)
   - A/B testing creatives
   - Daily optimization

3. **KOL Activation**
   - Content review & approval
   - Publishing coordination
   - Engagement monitoring

### Fase 4: Report & Optimization (Minggu 4+)
1. **Performance Tracking**
   - Daily metrics monitoring
   - Weekly optimization report
   - Budget reallocation (jika perlu)

2. **Campaign Report**
   - Full metrics vs KPI
   - Learning & insights
   - Recommendations for next campaign

## Template Campaign Brief

```markdown
# Campaign Brief: [Nama Kampanye]

## Overview
- **Klien:** [Nama Klien]
- **Periode:** [Tanggal Mulai - Tanggal Selesai]
- **Budget Total:** [Rp xxx]

## Objective
- Primary: [Contoh: Meningkatkan leads WhatsApp 30%]
- Secondary: [Contoh: Brand awareness di target area baru]

## Target Audience
- **Demografi:** [Usia, gender, lokasi]
- **Psikografi:** [Minat, perilaku, pain points]
- **Behavioral:** [Platform usage, purchase behavior]

## Key Message
- **Headline:** [Tagline kampanye]
- **Subheadline:** [Supporting message]
- **CTA:** [Call to action utama]

## Channel & Budget Allocation
| Channel | Budget | Objective |
|---|---|---|
| Meta Ads | Rp xxx | Leads/Traffic |
| KOL | Rp xxx | Awareness |
| Content Production | Rp xxx | Engagement |

## KPI
| Metrik | Target | Baseline |
|---|---|---|
| Leads | xxx | xxx |
| Reach | xxx | xxx |
| CTR | xxx% | xxx% |
| CPL | Rp xxx | Rp xxx |

## Timeline
| Minggu | Aktivitas |
|---|---|
| 1 | Research & Brief |
| 2 | Creative Development |
| 3 | Activation |
| 4 | Optimization & Report |
```

## Checklist Pre-Launch
- [ ] Campaign brief approved oleh klien
- [ ] Semua creative assets final
- [ ] Landing page live & tested
- [ ] Pixel/tracking terpasang
- [ ] Ads account sudah di-setup
- [ ] KOL sudah confirmed & briefed
- [ ] Content calendar finalized
- [ ] Budget sudah dialokasikan
