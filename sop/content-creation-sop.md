# SOP: Content Creation & Social Media Management

## Tujuan
Menghasilkan konten yang engaging, relevan, dan sesuai brand voice untuk semua platform sosial media klien.

## Alur Kerja Bulanan

### Minggu 1: Planning
1. Review performa bulan sebelumnya
2. Identifikasi momen/event bulan ini (hari besar, promo, musim)
3. Buat Content Calendar (tanggal, platform, jenis konten, topic)
4. Approval dari klien

### Minggu 2-3: Production
1. Shooting foto/video (jika diperlukan on-site)
2. Editing visual (graphic design, video editing)
3. Copywriting (caption, hashtag, CTA)
4. Review internal oleh Content Director

### Minggu 4: Publishing & Monitoring
1. Scheduling konten
2. Daily monitoring engagement
3. Community management (reply comments, DM)
4. Dokumentasi performa konten

## Jenis Konten per Platform

### Instagram Feed
| Jenis | Deskripsi | Frekuensi |
|---|---|---|
| Single Image | Foto produk/suasana | 3-5x/bulan |
| Carousel | Edukasi, tips, behind the scene | 3-5x/bulan |
| Reels | Video pendek, storytelling, ambience | 5-7x/bulan |

### Instagram Stories
| Jenis | Deskripsi | Frekuensi |
|---|---|---|
| Daily Update | Info terkini, promo hari ini | Daily |
| Poll/Quiz | Engagement booster | 2-3x/minggu |
| Repost UGC | User generated content | As needed |
| Behind the Scene | Proses kerja, tim | 1-2x/minggu |

### TikTok
| Jenis | Deskripsi | Frekuensi |
|---|---|---|
| Ambience Video | Suasana lokasi, cinematic | 3-4x/bulan |
| Trending Audio | Ikut tren dengan twist brand | 2-3x/bulan |
| Storytelling | Narasi pengalaman | 2-3x/bulan |
| Tutorial/Tips | Konten edukasi ringan | 1-2x/bulan |

## Caption Guidelines

### Struktur Caption:
1. **Hook** (1 kalimat pertama yang menarik)
2. **Body** (informasi utama, storytelling)
3. **CTA** (ajakan bertindak)
4. **Hashtag** (mix branded + niche + trending)

### Contoh Hook Styles:
- Pertanyaan: "Pernah nggak sih ngerasa butuh healing tapi males jauh-jauh?"
- Statement bold: "Ini bukan Bali. Ini Garut."
- Relatable moment: "Weekend tanpa rencana? Kita punya ide."
- Data shocking: "77% tamu kami bilang ini tempat terbaik di Garut."

### Hashtag Strategy:
- 3-5 branded hashtags
- 5-10 niche hashtags
- 3-5 trending/location hashtags
- Total: 15-20 hashtags per post

## Visual Guidelines
- Pastikan konsistensi color tone
- Gunakan template brand untuk carousel
- Resolusi minimum: 1080x1080 (feed), 1080x1920 (story/reels)
- Watermark/logo placement: konsisten di semua konten

## Approval Flow
1. Content Creator → Content Director (review)
2. Content Director → Marketing Strategist (alignment)
3. Marketing Strategist → Klien (final approval)
4. Klien approved → Scheduling

## Tools yang Digunakan
- Canva / Adobe (design)
- CapCut / Premiere (video editing)
- Later / Meta Business Suite (scheduling)
- Google Sheets (content calendar)
