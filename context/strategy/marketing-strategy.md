# Strategi Marketing Umum — Moté Kreatif

## Framework Marketing Mote

### Funnel yang Digunakan:
```
Brand Awareness → Consideration → Action → Purchase
```

### Metrik per Funnel:

#### Brand Awareness
- Content Impression
- Audience Reach
- Follower Growth

#### Consideration
- Post Interaction
- Engagement Rate by Reach
- Audience Follow

#### Action
- Click Links
- Click Through Rate (CTR)
- Page View (Landing Page View)

#### Purchase
- Add To Cart (OTA)
- Lead (WhatsApp)
- Cost Per Lead (CPL)

### Profitability Marketing
- Real Omset (Revenue)
- Marketing Spent
- Marketing Cost as % of Revenue

## Platform yang Dikelola

### Owned Media
- **Instagram** — Feed post, Reels, Stories, Carousel
- **TikTok** — Short video content
- **Website/Landing Page** — Untuk konversi

### Paid Media
- **Meta Ads** (Facebook & Instagram Ads) — Objective: Traffic, Leads, Awareness
- **Google Ads** — Search, Display (beberapa klien)
- **KOL Activation** — Influencer marketing lokal & nasional

### Earned Media
- **Google Reviews** — Monitoring sentimen positif & negatif
- **User Generated Content** — Dari KOL dan tamu/pelanggan

## Jenis Konten yang Diproduksi

### Instagram
- Feed Post (Single Image / Carousel)
- Reels (Short Video)
- Stories (Daily content)
- Rata-rata: 12-15 feed post/bulan, 90 stories/bulan

### TikTok
- Short Video (10-12 video/bulan)
- Fokus: Ambience, Behind the Scene, Storytelling

### Konten Ads
- Visual promosi produk/layanan
- Landing page specific campaigns
- Retargeting creatives

## Reporting Standard

### Laporan Bulanan (Monthly Report) mencakup:
1. **Executive Summary** — 3 poin utama
2. **Marketing Performance Dashboard** — Ringkasan metrik funnel
3. **Owned Media Performance** — Per platform (IG, TikTok)
4. **Paid Media Performance** — Ads & KOL
5. **Google Review Analysis** — Sentimen positif & negatif
6. **Key Learning** — Learning, Challenge, Strategy (3 kolom)

### KPI yang Dilaporkan:
- Revenue vs Marketing Cost ratio
- Engagement Rate
- Reach & Impression growth
- Lead generation & CPL
- Page Views & CTR
- Follower Growth
