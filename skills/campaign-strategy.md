# Skill: Campaign Strategy Planner

## Deskripsi
Merancang strategi kampanye marketing terintegrasi dari riset hingga eksekusi, termasuk target audiens, channel mix, budget allocation, dan timeline.

## Kapan Digunakan
- Merencanakan kampanye baru untuk klien
- Seasonal campaign planning (Lebaran, Natal, Tahun Baru, dll)
- Product launch campaign
- Brand repositioning
- Promo/event activation

## Framework Strategi

### 1. Situation Analysis
```markdown
## Analisis Situasi

### Brand Audit
- Posisi brand saat ini
- Kekuatan & kelemahan
- Performa channel existing

### Market Analysis
- Tren industri terkini
- Perilaku target audiens
- Seasonal patterns

### Competitive Landscape
- Kompetitor langsung (3-5 brand)
- Diferensiasi vs kompetitor
- Gap & opportunity
```

### 2. Campaign Objective (SMART)
```markdown
## Objective

### Primary Objective
- Specific: [apa yang ingin dicapai]
- Measurable: [metrik yang diukur]
- Achievable: [berdasarkan baseline data]
- Relevant: [selaras dengan tujuan bisnis]
- Time-bound: [dalam periode berapa]

### Secondary Objectives
- [objective 2]
- [objective 3]
```

### 3. Audience Targeting
```markdown
## Target Audience

### Primary Audience
- **Demografi:** Usia, gender, lokasi, income level
- **Psikografi:** Minat, nilai, lifestyle
- **Pain Points:** Masalah yang brand bisa selesaikan
- **Media Consumption:** Platform, waktu aktif
- **Purchase Behavior:** Decision triggers, barriers

### Secondary Audience
- [audience 2 details]

### Audience Persona
- **Nama:** [persona name]
- **Usia:** [range]
- **Pekerjaan:** [job]
- **Quote:** "[apa yang mereka pikirkan tentang kategori ini]"
- **Preferred Platform:** [platform]
- **Decision Driver:** [apa yang mendorong mereka memilih]
```

### 4. Strategic Approach
```markdown
## Strategi

### Positioning Statement
Untuk [target audience] yang [kebutuhan/keinginan],
[brand] adalah [kategori] yang [diferensiasi utama]
karena [reason to believe].

### Key Message Hierarchy
1. **Primary Message:** [pesan utama]
2. **Supporting Messages:** [3-4 pesan pendukung]
3. **Proof Points:** [bukti/data/testimoni]

### Campaign Narrative
- **Tagline:** [tagline kampanye]
- **Story Arc:** [alur cerita kampanye]
- **Emotional Hook:** [emosi yang ingin ditrigger]
```

### 5. Channel Strategy & Budget
```markdown
## Channel Mix & Budget

### Allocation Framework
| Channel | % Budget | Objective | KPI |
|---------|----------|-----------|-----|
| Content Production | 20% | Quality assets | Post count |
| Meta Ads | 40% | Leads & Traffic | CPL, CTR |
| KOL Activation | 25% | Awareness | Reach, ER |
| Google Ads | 15% | Search intent | CPC, Conv |

### Content Distribution
| Platform | Post/Week | Jenis Konten | Tujuan |
|----------|-----------|--------------|--------|
| IG Feed | 3-4x | Carousel, Reels | Engagement |
| IG Story | Daily | BTS, Promo | Retention |
| TikTok | 3x | Short video | Discovery |
| Meta Ads | Ongoing | Image, Video | Conversion |
```

### 6. Timeline & Milestones
```markdown
## Timeline

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Minggu 1-2)
- [ ] Market research completed
- [ ] Campaign brief finalized
- [ ] Creative assets produced
- [ ] KOL outreach & briefing
- [ ] Landing page live

### Phase 2: Launch (Minggu 3-4)
- [ ] Organic content go-live
- [ ] Paid ads activated
- [ ] KOL content published
- [ ] Community engagement active

### Phase 3: Optimization (Minggu 5-6)
- [ ] Performance review (weekly)
- [ ] A/B test winners scaled
- [ ] Budget reallocation
- [ ] Mid-campaign report

### Phase 4: Wrap-up (Minggu 7-8)
- [ ] Final performance pull
- [ ] Campaign report
- [ ] Key learnings documented
- [ ] Next campaign recommendations
```
