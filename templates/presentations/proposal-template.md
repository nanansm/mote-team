## Kode Dokumen

### Format Nomor:
`MOTE.[KODE_LAYANAN].[DD-MM]/[YY]`

### Kode Layanan:
| Kode | Layanan |
|------|---------|
| DM | Digital Marketing (Full) |
| SMMO | Social Media & Marketplace Optimization |
| TDMO | Team Development & Marketing Optimization |
| BG | Brand Growth |
| CA | Campaign Activation |

### Contoh:
- `MOTE.DM.26-05/25` — Digital Marketing, 26 Mei 2025
- `MOTE.SMMO.06-04/26` — Socmed & Marketplace, 6 April 2026
- `MOTE.TDMO.27-03/26` — Team Dev, 27 Maret 2026
